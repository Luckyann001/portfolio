About Me

I am a deeply curious and driven technology enthusiast with a growing focus on how digital systems are designed, built, secured, and scaled. My journey began with software engineering, where I developed a strong appreciation for clean code, structured problem-solving, and user-centered design.

As my interest expanded, I became drawn to cybersecurity—understanding how systems fail, how vulnerabilities are exploited, and how technology can be protected against evolving threats. In parallel, I am exploring artificial intelligence and computer science fundamentals to strengthen my analytical thinking and deepen my understanding of how intelligent systems work at a core level.

Beyond technical ability, I value discipline, clarity, adaptability, and continuous learning. I approach challenges with intention, balance creativity with logic, and believe technology should be both powerful and responsible. This portfolio reflects not just what I can build today, but the direction I am deliberately growing toward.
